Eye Shape Liberty Count

1-space = 1 liberty · 2-space = 2 liberties 3-space = 3 liberties · 4-space = 5 liberties 5-space = 8 liberties · 6-space = 12 liberties

When enemy stones are inside the eye shape, subtract the number of enemy stones from the above count to get the liberty count.
